Introduction

This website is inspired by the "Accidentally Wes Anderson" book by Wally Koval, which features photographs taken from around the world that emulate the vivid and unique film aesthetic of the American filmmaker Wes Anderson. Instead of incorporating images taken directly from the book, a few free images from Unsplash contributors were included for the purposes of this assignment.
